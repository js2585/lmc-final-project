Key Differences

  • Texture Inconsistencies: AI images may have unnatural textures.
  • Lighting Issues: Look for inconsistent lighting and shadows.
  • Anatomical Errors: AI might generate distorted body parts.
  • Background Artifacts: Unusual patterns or artifacts may appear.
